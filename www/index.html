Welcome

Linkin Park is an American rock band formed in Los Angeles in 1996. Composed until July 20, 2017, of Chester Bennington, Mike Shinoda, Brad Delson, Phoenix, Rob Bourdon, and Joe Hahn, they are one of the most commercially successful bands of the 2000s and 2010s, having sold over 100 million records worldwide, 70 million of which are albums.

In their early career, the group contributed to the success of the nu metal genre, yet offered a more accessible version of it in their style, thus often being compared to pop. Linkin Park has won numerous awards, including two Grammy Awards. Their albums have been certified gold and platinum multiple times in the United States, and their debut album Hybrid Theory was certified diamond in the same country for selling over 12 million copies. Furthermore, they were named by MTV in 2003 as the sixth greatest band of all time.
